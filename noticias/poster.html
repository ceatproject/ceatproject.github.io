[POSTER]: Regulatory Framework for Innovation Assessment in Advanced Therapies in Europe.

Ángela Ponce Polo, Álvaro Ritoré Hidalgo, Roke Iñaki Oruezabal Guijarro

ESGCT organises an Annual Congress, which brings together scientists working in the fields of gene and cell therapy from throughout Europe and beyond. The ESGCT 2019 was held in Barcelona during the days 22-25 of October. A total of 1793 attendees from different part of the world (US, Europe and South Korea) and different sectors (academia, industry, students…) joined the meeting. The Andalusian Network for the design & translation of Advanced Therapies (And&tAT), partner of the CEAT project, presented a poster entitled “Regulatory Framework for Innovation Assessment in Advanced Therapies in Europe”, in which they displayed the Approval & Reimbuserment Criteria of different European agencies with a special focus on the innovation assessment of the drugs.
